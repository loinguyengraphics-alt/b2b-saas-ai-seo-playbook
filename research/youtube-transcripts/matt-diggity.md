### Core Strategy: Reddit as "AI Food"
AI models currently trust Reddit more than almost any other unique source. By strategically positioning a brand within relevant Reddit threads, B2B marketers can feed their content directly into the datasets of AI search engines, dominating AI Overviews and driving massive referral traffic.

### The Step-by-Step Reddit Marketing Workflow
1. **Build a "Non-Marketer" Profile:** 
   Avoid polished bios, company logos as avatars, and external links. Keep the profile looking like a normal, authentic user to avoid immediate bans from moderators.
2. **Lurk and Learn (The AI Shortcut):** 
   Spend 7-14 days understanding community rules and tone. *Prompt Engineering Tip:* Drop the subreddit URL into ChatGPT or Claude and ask it to summarize the rules, tone, and what typically gets upvoted to create an instant community playbook.
3. **The Two-Tier Subreddit Strategy:** 
   * *Tier 1:* Start in smaller niche communities to build karma, rhythm, and recognition.
   * *Tier 2:* Move to larger subreddits once account history is established.
   * *SEO Alignment:* Search high-value keywords on Google and identify which subreddits are already ranking on Page 1. These are the exact threads AI systems are actively reading from.
4. **Engage in Trending Threads:** 
   Filter posts by "Rising" or "Hot". Be early to comment, answer questions with real expertise, or correct misconceptions to maximize visibility and secure the top answer spot.
5. **Write "Upvote-Winning" Comments (6 Frameworks):** 
   Format heavily for scannability (bullet points, clear line breaks) because LLMs prefer structured data. Use these styles:
   * *The Straight Shooter:* Direct answers with actionable steps (no fluff).
   * *The Missing Piece:* Adding one vital detail missing from existing organic answers.
   * *The Story Drop:* Real-world experiences and lessons learned.
   * *The Proof Point:* Providing hard data, stats, links to studies, or screenshots.
   * *The Mini Playbook:* Step-by-step checklists.
   * *The Brand Voice:* Used extremely sparingly, only when the company is organically mentioned by others.
6. **Create Dedicated "AI Food" Threads:** 
   Publish case studies, teardowns, and free templates utilizing clear headings and numbers that LLMs can easily parse, scrape, and cite as authority sources.

### Key Takeaway
B2B SaaS brands can no longer rely solely on their owned domains. They must infiltrate third-party community platforms like Reddit. By acting like a genuine user and structuring answers clearly for machine parsing, brands can achieve exponential growth in AI referral visibility (e.g., generating a 2,814% increase in AI traffic for a real estate client).
